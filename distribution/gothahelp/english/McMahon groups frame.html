McMahon groups frame

Use this frame to set the McMahon bar and to define which player will be a member of Top/super groups
A guide to Setting MacMahon groups is in : http://www.eurogofed.org/gotour/rules.htm


Players whose rank equals the McMahon bar are initially placed into the Top Group and shown in the Bar (Top Group) panel.
Players whose rank equals the McMahon bar - 1 are initially shown in the Bar - 1 panel.

You can then move players from one group to another one by using arrow buttons.
